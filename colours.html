Usage Guidelines

Primary Colours

Primary Green is our main brand colour and is used for primary styling elements. Our other green tones and used for supporting colors.

Secondary Colours

Secondary colours should be used sparingly to highlight important information, success messages, warnings, or to create visual interest. They do not compete, the primary colours.

Neutral Colours

Neutral colours are use for text and background elements, and White for card backgrounds and content areas.

Accessibility

Always ensure sufficient contrast between text and background colours to meet accessibility standards.
