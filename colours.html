Usage Guidelines

Primary Colours

Primary Blue is our main brand colour and should be used for primary buttons, links, and key UI elements. Dark Blue can be used for hover states and Light Blue for backgrounds and highlights.

Secondary Colours

Secondary colours should be used sparingly to highlight important information, success messages, warnings, or to create visual interest. They should complement, not compete with, the primary colours.

Neutral Colours

Neutral colours form the foundation of our interface. Use Dark for text, Grey for secondary text and borders, Light Grey for backgrounds, and White for card backgrounds and content areas.

Accessibility

Always ensure sufficient contrast between text and background colours to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text).
