Photography Guidelines

Photography Example 1

Natural & Authentic

Use natural-looking photos that feel authentic rather than overly staged. Aim for genuine expressions and situations that our audience can relate to.

Photography Example 2

Consistent Editing

Apply a consistent editing style to all photography. Our photos use natural lighting with slightly increased brightness and contrast, and a subtle warm tone.

Photography Example 3

Diversity & Inclusion

Ensure our imagery represents diverse people, backgrounds, and situations. Our visuals should be inclusive and accessible to all.

Charts & Data Visualization

Data visualizations should be clear, accessible, and consistent with our brand style.

Chart Example: Bar Chart

Bar & Column Charts

  • Use primary brand colors for main data series
  • Include proper labels and legends
  • Keep designs minimal with no unnecessary decoration
  • Use consistent spacing between bars
Chart Example: Line Chart

Line & Area Charts

  • Use 2-3px line thickness for visibility
  • Include data points at key intervals
  • Use light grid lines to guide the eye
  • Consider using gradient fills for area charts
Chart Example: Pie Chart
