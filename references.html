Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get access to the design files?

Design files can be accessed through our shared design library. Contact the design team to request access or visit the Design Assets link above.

Can I suggest new components or design patterns?

Yes! We encourage collaboration. Please submit your suggestions through our feedback form or contact the design team directly.

How often is the design system updated?

We release updates quarterly with minor patches as needed. Check the changelog in our documentation for the latest updates.

Where can I report bugs or issues?

Issues can be reported through our GitHub repository or by contacting the development team directly.

Is there a Figma/Sketch/XD library available?

Yes, we maintain component libraries in multiple design tools. Access these through the Design Assets section.
